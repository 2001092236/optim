# Uncategorized

1. Show, that these conditions are equivalent:
    
    $$
     \|\nabla f(x) - \nabla f(z) \| \le L \|x-z\| 
    $$
    
    and
    
    $$
    f(z) \le f(x) + \nabla f(x)^T(z-x) + \frac L 2 \|z-x\|^2
    $$
